PesisirKita Field App

A Progressive Web App (PWA) for direct field action data input. Equipped with four main modules:

  • Planting Module: Record GIS coordinates, seedling types, and photos of mangrove conditions.
  • Cleanup Module: Report the weight (Kg) and type of coastal debris collected by volunteers/students.
  • Observation Module: Monitor wildlife activity, flora, and ecosystem health indicators in the vicinity.
  • Early Signal Module: Report coastal anomalies, tidal flood early warnings, and infrastructure damage in real-time.
Open Data Input App
Live

Command Center Dashboard

A visual monitoring center integrating all field signals into distributed intelligence. Monitor ecosystem conditions holistically through a single control gateway:

  • Geographical Visualization (GeoJSON): Detailed mapping of mangrove areas using satellite polygons for accurate real-time land area monitoring and reporting point distribution.
  • Historical Trend Analysis: A comparative algorithm analyzing whether local environmental conditions are improving, stagnant, or require immediate intervention based on log history.
  • Anomaly Detection System: The dashboard automatically highlights Early Warning signals such as sea-level rise or infrastructure damage for rapid response coordination.

Why is PesisirKita Different?

Functional comparison of PesisirKita against conventional environmental reporting or field survey apps.

Conventional Apps

  • Rigid Implementation: Setting up new systems takes a long time and requires complex infrastructure installations in each region.
  • Siloed Data (Passive): Reports only go to an admin's desk or a closed dashboard, inaccessible to the public/schools transparently.
  • Single Feature: Usually focuses on only one thing (e.g., logging trash only, or planting trees only).
New Innovation

PesisirKita

  • Rapid Deployment: Lightweight architecture (PWA & CDN) allows platform activation in new coastal areas within hours without technical hurdles.
  • Distributed Intelligence: Turns reporters (students/citizens) into "Social Sensors", data instantly appears on public Leaderboards and Dashboards.
  • 4-in-1 Ecosystem: Combines Cleanups, Planting, Observation, and Early Warning in a single unified tactical platform.
